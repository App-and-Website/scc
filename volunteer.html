Volunteer with Southern Communities Council (SCC)

At Southern Communities Council (SCC), we believe that together we can make a difference. Volunteers play an essential role in advancing our mission to empower marginalised voices from the Global South and promote social justice through sustainable initiatives and inclusive practices. By lending your time, skills, and passion, you will help us amplify the voices of those who need it most and contribute to building a more equitable and inclusive world.

Why Volunteer with SCC?

Our volunteers are an integral part of the team, contributing to a range of impactful activities. Whether youâ€™re fundraising, assisting with events, supporting our social media campaigns, or offering your professional expertise, your involvement directly helps strengthen communities, uplift individuals, and drive positive social change.

Areas to Volunteer

We welcome volunteers with a variety of skills, from technical expertise to passion for social justice. Here are a few ways you can get involved:

  • Community Development: Help support and build sustainable programs that address economic, social, and cultural needs in the Global South.
  • Advocacy & Outreach: Amplify the voices of the marginalized by assisting with awareness campaigns, media outreach, and community education initiatives.
  • Event Support: Help coordinate or assist at events, workshops, and gatherings that aim to promote empowerment, connection, and collaboration.
  • Social Media & Communications: Share your digital skills to create content, manage our social media, and engage online communities.
  • Professional Expertise: Lend your specialized skills in areas such as IT, legal services, healthcare, and education to strengthen our programs and initiatives.
