Southern Communities Council: Empowering Global South Voices

The Southern Communities Council (SCC) is a dedicated advocacy, networking, and support platform committed to uplifting the voices and rights of individuals and communities from the Global South. Our mission is rooted in principles of ecology, people, and peace, which are woven into all our initiatives and actions. SCC aims to empower those impacted by both historical colonialism and current-day discriminatory practices in Australia, fostering an inclusive and equitable society for all.

SCC Services

Providing a diverse range of culturally appropriate services that are mindful of Aboriginal traditions and values, encompassing but not limited to the following:

Aboriginal Cultural & Linguistic Revival

We work to revitalise Aboriginal languages and cultural practices, fostering pride and continuity in cultural heritage within Aboriginal communities.

Aboriginal Initiatives

Led by Aboriginal leaders on our board, these initiatives focus on empowering Aboriginal communities by amplifying their voices, promoting their rights, and addressing their unique challenges.

Community & Economic Development

SCC supports community-driven economic projects that build selfsufficiency, increase employment opportunities, and strengthen local economies.

Care Advice and Networks

We provide culturally sensitive guidance and create supportive networks to assist individuals and families in navigating healthcare, legal, and social services.

Professional Development

Our programs focus on skill-building, career growth, and networking opportunities, empowering individuals to thrive professionally and contribute meaningfully to their communities.

Community Copyrights

SCC advocates for the protection of cultural knowledge, ensuring that communities retain ownership of their intellectual and cultural property.

Social Connections

We foster social bonds within and across communities through networking events, support groups, and activities that encourage shared experiences and collaboration.

Social Enterprise

We support social enterprises that address community needs, create sustainable job opportunities, and reinvest profits into local development andsocial causes.

Co-operatives

SCC promotes co-operatives as community-owned businesses, helping members share resources, generate income, and work together toward common economic goals.

Young People

Through youth-focused programs, we engage young people in leadership, education, & community activities, inspiring the next generation to become advocates.

Homelessness

We assist homelessness by providing resources, connections to services, and advocating for affordable housing and supportive programs needed in the community.
